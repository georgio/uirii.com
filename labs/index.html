This Lab focuses on exploring different ways of expression through movement, voice and object manipulation.

Throughout the sessions, participants are invited to search within the elementary components of choreography such as form, rhythm, gesture and tempo; find ways to transform themselves into words, shapes, images, concepts, ideas and thoughts using movement and sound. Using exercises from dance, physical theater and object manipulation, each participant will be encouraged to find his/her own vocabulary.

The Lab is for people who are interested to deepen their perception of dance/theatre composition and for those who just want to play and discover alternate ways of expressing themselves.
