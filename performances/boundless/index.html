While Homo sapiens dwells in the cave of its mind trying to escape its boundaries, constructing ingenious plans to reach immortality through genetic engineering and artificial intelligence, a little immortal jellyfish, with no brain and no heart, thrusts itself quietly around the oceans. What will Homo sapiens do once it reaches immortality? How will it cope with its new transformation? Will it wander in harmony like the jellyfish? Will the angst liquefy or new anxieties will emerge? What is this restless species looking for..?
